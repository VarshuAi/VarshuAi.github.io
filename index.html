About Me

I build because I can't stop being curious.

I'm Varshan Gowda SR, a self-taught builder based in Bengaluru. I don't follow a single lane — I jump between ideas, tools, and projects driven purely by curiosity and the urge to create something real. From scripts and apps to utilities and experiments, if it interests me, I build it.

With over 204 public repositories and a growing community of 136 followers on GitHub, I'm always shipping something new. My work spans Python, JavaScript, Rust, and beyond — whatever the project calls for.

I believe the best way to learn is to build, break, and rebuild. Every repo is a story. Every commit is a step forward.
